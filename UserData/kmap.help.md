## Default-macbook - bbc micro - POSITIONAL MAPPING

For a full size keyboard INS and DEL which are in the middle could be used for the missing keys on row 1 and 2.
But for small keyboards, then ± and ~ will need to be used, or F11.  F12 seems to be used for BREAK always!
